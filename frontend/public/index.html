💡 What We Built

LibraSense solves a real problem at SRM KTR — students walk all the way to the library only to find no seats available. Our system lets students check seat availability in real time, book seats in advance, and check in via QR codes — all from their phones.

📍
Real-Time Tracking
Live seat status across 3 floors, auto-refreshed every 10 seconds
📅
Advance Booking
Reserve seats up to 2 hours ahead with auto-expiry
📱
QR Check-In
Scan seat QR code for instant check-in, no manual entry needed
📊
Admin Analytics
Usage stats, noise control, announcements and full override
